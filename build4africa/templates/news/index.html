Youth Digital Empowerment Program Launch

Training 10,000 Young Africans in Future-Ready Digital Skills We're proud to launch the Youth Digital Empowerment Program (YDEP) — a transformative initiative to equip 10,000 young Africans (ages 16–35) with essential digital skills for the future workforce. Training areas include:

  • Web & Mobile Development
  • Data & AI
  • Cybersecurity
  • Digital Marketing
  • Cloud & DevOps

    • ✅ weeks of hybrid training
    ✅ Certification, mentorship, job access
    ✅ Free to join – no prior experience needed

    West Africa Innovation Hub Recognition

    Build4Africa has been honored with a prestigious award recognizing its excellence in technology innovation and community impact across West Africa. This accolade reflects our unwavering commitment to empowering youth, bridging the digital divide, and fostering inclusive growth through cutting-edge training, grassroots initiatives, and transformative tech programs. As a recognized innovation hub, Build4Africa continues to lead efforts in shaping a digitally empowered and socially resilient future for African communities.Build4Africa is proud to be at the forefront of Africa’s innovation movement—driving progress, inclusion, and opportunity at scale.

    Strategic Partnership with Build4Africa Foundation

    Build4Africa is proud to announce a new partnership to expand digital access and inclusion across rural communities in five African nations. This collaboration brings together leaders in education, technology, and grassroots development to deliver scalable, high-impact digital empowerment. Through shared resources, mobile tech hubs, and localized training, the initiative will equip underserved populations—especially youth and women—with essential digital skills, internet access, and economic opportunities. Together, we aim to bridge the digital divide and build lasting pathways to innovation and inclusion across the continent.
